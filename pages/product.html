About PP/HDPE Woven Fabric Rolls

We manufacture high strength PP/HDPE woven fabric rolls used in sacks, bags, and lamination processes. Available in multiple widths and GSM. Custom printing and lamination services available.

Key Specifications

  • Material: PP / HDPE
  • Width: 24" - 120" (Custom)
  • GSM: 40 - 450
  • Lamination: HDPE / PE / BOPP (optional)

Applications

Used widely for packaging of food grains, fertilizers, chemicals, cement, and more.
